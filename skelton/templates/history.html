AMGEC - HISTORY

The AMGEC conference was launched with its first edition held online, a necessary adaptation during the COVID-19 pandemic. Building on this success, the committee organized the second edition in person in Sousse in November 2022, marking a highly anticipated return to physical gatherings. The event's momentum was confirmed with the third edition, also held in Sousse in November 2023, solidifying its status as an essential annual meeting for the scientific community.
