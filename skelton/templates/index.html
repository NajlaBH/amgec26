Bridging the Gap between Research and Industry

The Research and Technology Centre of Energy (CRTEn) organizes the fourth edition of the advanced materials and Green energy conference (AMGEC2026)

We welcome you to AMGEC 2026 which will be held, in Tunisia, on March 26th-29th, 2026. The purpose of the conference is to present the most important results coming from research activities in materials and green energy technologies and systems. The conference intends to draw the pathway for researchers, academics and industrial to come together and face challenges in the fields of advanced materials and renewable energy.
